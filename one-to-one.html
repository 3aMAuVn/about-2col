One-to-One Tutoring provides individualised maths support tailored to each student’s learning needs, pace, and goals in a supportive, pressure-free environment.

🎯

Best For

  • Students who need individual attention and targeted support
  • Children lacking confidence or feeling anxious about maths
  • Students filling learning gaps or keeping up with schoolwork
  • Learners who want to move ahead of the curriculum
  • Students preparing for NAPLAN, selective entry, or VCE
🧠

What Students Do

  • Work on carefully selected problems matched to their level
  • Learn concepts step by step with clear explanations
  • Ask questions freely in a safe, supportive setting
  • Review mistakes and improve problem-solving strategies
  • Practise key skills until confident and secure
  • Apply learning to schoolwork, assessments, and exams
🗂️

Program Information

  • Lessons fully personalised using ongoing diagnostic assessment
  • Pace adjusted continuously to suit the student
  • Focus on support, consolidation, extension, or exam preparation
  • Personalised materials and practice tasks provided
  • Regular feedback shared with students and parents where appropriate
  • Available online or face-to-face depending on location
⭐

Benefits

  • Faster academic progress through targeted instruction
  • Stronger conceptual understanding
  • Greater confidence and independence in maths
  • Reduced anxiety and frustration
  • Improved performance in school assessments and exams
