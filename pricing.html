🎁 Discounts & rewards

We aim to support families and recognise students’ effort, progress, and growth.

💛 1. Block Booking Discount (Tutoring)

A 10% discount applies when you book a block of 10 or more tutoring lessons and pay in advance.

  • Available for one-to-one and group lessons.
  • Helps families plan ahead and reduce overall costs.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 2. Family Discounts

To support families with more than one child learning with us:

  • Second child: 10% discount
  • Third child: 20% discount
Example – 3 children

Child A: highest fee → no family discount
Child B: middle fee → 10% off
Child C: lowest fee → 20% off

Max discount per child per period: 20%.

💬 3. Support for Families

If your budget is limited, please contact us — we may be able to match students into small groups or suggest options.

  • Small groups can reduce cost while keeping quality high.
  • We can recommend the most efficient plan for your goals.

🏅 4. Progress & Achievement Rewards

Students may receive up to 10% discount on the next billing period’s tuition fees.

  • Maintaining high performance – 5%: consistently strong results.
  • Improving school grades – 5%: visible improvement (e.g., C→B, B→A).
  • Showing steady progress – 5%: confidence, accuracy, reasoning, engagement.

If multiple criteria are met, the reward is capped at 10%.

🔎 5. How Discounts Are Applied

  • Only one type of discount applies per child per billing period.
  • If multiple discounts apply, the highest one is used.
  • The maximum discount per child is 20% per billing cycle.
